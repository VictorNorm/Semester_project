The museum is closed until 1st January.

We are hoping to return to our normal opening times as of the begining of 2021.

During the school holidays the museum is open the full week, Sunday will have the same opening times as Saturday. Please note that the galleries close 30 minutes before the museum closes.

During these special pandemic times it is of utmost importance that all visitors abide by the museums rules.

Children under the age of 12 must be accompanied by an adult.

Children in the ages 13-16 must provide contact details of their parents or guardians in line with the test and trace initiative set up by the government.

Visitors should at all times keep at least 1m distance from other visitors.

Our number one priority is the safety and health of our visitors. The museum will otherwise operate as usual until other changes might be necessary.

Booking is required for entry.

Because of covid-19 all visitors must book a free admission ticket to gain entry to the museum.
